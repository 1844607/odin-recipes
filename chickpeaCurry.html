Chickpea Curry

Melt-in-your-mouth chickpeas in a delicious curry. Can be made in just 45 minuts, and serves a family of 4

  • 1 pound of dry chickpeas
  • dash of oil for the veggies
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 32oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 12 fl oz can of coconut milk
  • 4 tsp curry powder
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp tumeric powder
  • 1 tsp salt
    1. Add Chickpeas and plenty of water to the instant pot. Press the 'Beans/Chili' setting and hit 'Start'
    2. Add your chopped Onion & Garlic to your pot and any other veggies, to taste
    3. When your veggies are nearly cooked to your preference, add the coconut milk and crushed tomatoes, then spices. Allow to simmer until the chickpeas are ready.
    4. Add chickpeas and continue to simmer while you cook your rice in the instant pot, then serve!
